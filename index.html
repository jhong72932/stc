Q1 2026: Building the Foundation

 

Building The Foundation Q1 2026

In the first half of the year, StreamChain's core engineering team is focused on stabilizing Stream Mainnet 2.0 — the project's independent blockchain infrastructure capable of processing transactions in under one second at an average gas fee of just $0.001. Alongside mainnet hardening, the team is rolling out significant upgrades to the Stream Wallet, expanding functionality to include enhanced staking, mileage conversion, multi-asset management, and ticketing services — all from a single interface. These foundational steps, the company says, are critical to ensuring the platform can handle the real-world transaction volumes expected in the second half of the year.
